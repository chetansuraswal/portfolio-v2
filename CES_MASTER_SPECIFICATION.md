---

# 1. Purpose

The CES (Chetan Editing System) Master Specification is the single source of truth for the Chetan Suraswal brand.

This document defines every creative, technical, and strategic standard that governs the CES ecosystem.

Every decision related to design, editing, branding, storytelling, website development, motion, sound, typography, color, and future products must align with this specification.

This document exists to eliminate inconsistency.

If a future decision conflicts with this specification, the specification takes priority.

---

# 2. Vision

Build one of the world's most respected premium video editing brands.

The goal is not to become known only in India or Asia.

The goal is to become globally recognized for creating premium cinematic edits that combine precision, emotion, storytelling, and timeless design.

The CES brand should become synonymous with quality.

---

# 3. Mission

Create films and digital experiences that people remember.

Every project should communicate intention rather than complexity.

The objective is not to impress people with effects.

The objective is to move people through storytelling, rhythm, composition, sound, motion, and thoughtful editing.

Every project released under the CES identity must satisfy these standards.

✓ Strong Storytelling

✓ Professional Sound Design
