# ABOUT SECTION

About

## Description

I'm Chetan Suraswal, a premium video editor focused on cinematic storytelling, commercial visuals and luxury aesthetics.

My editing philosophy is simple:

Every frame should have purpose.

I believe exceptional editing isn't about using more effects.

It's about making viewers feel something.

Through the Chetan Editing System (CES), every project follows a consistent creative process built on precision, emotion and timeless design.
